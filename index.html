The Assyriologist 𒀝𒅗𒁺𒌑𒌝

A searchable reader for ~19,700 machine-translated Akkadian & Sumerian cuneiform texts. Search the English, filter by period, genre and site, and read the cuneiform alongside its draft translation.

⚠ These are unvetted machine translations — research leads, not citable scholarly readings. They are produced by fine-tuned language models and frequently contain errors, especially on broken or fragmentary tablets. Always verify against the cuneiform and the primary editions (eBL / ORACC) before relying on any reading.

Part of The Assyriologist — an Akkadian→English translation & analysis project (a three-part Wolfram Community series). Data: the electronic Babylonian Library (eBL) and ORACC; translations by fine-tuned LLMs (Gemma / Mistral). Cuneiform rendered with Noto Sans Cuneiform. Runs entirely in your browser (the whole SQLite database loads once via sql.js) — no server, nothing tracked.
